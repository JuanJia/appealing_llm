Here's the recipe for the Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry with Brown Rice:  Ingredients:  * 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breasts * 1 cup broccoli florets * 1 cup bell peppers (any color) * 1 cup snap peas * 1 cup carrots, diced * 1 cup brown rice, uncooked * 3 cups chicken broth * 2 tablespoons soy sauce * 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional) * 1 tablespoon cornstarch * 1 tablespoon vegetable oil * Salt and pepper, to taste  Instructions:  1. Cook the brown rice according to package instructions. Typically, combine 1 cup of brown rice with 3 cups of chicken broth in a saucepan, bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 45 minutes or until the rice is tender. Set aside. 2. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt and pepper. 3. Wash and chop the vegetables. 4. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), and cornstarch. Set aside. 5. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned and almost cooked through, about 4-5 minutes. Remove from the skillet and set aside. 6. In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil. Add the broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas, and carrots, and cook for 4-5 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender but still crisp. 7. Return the chicken to the skillet with the vegetables. Add the sauce mixture and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened. 8. Serve the stir-fry over a bed of brown rice and enjoy!  Feel free to adjust the ingredients or seasonings to your taste. This dish is versatile and can be customized with your favorite vegetables or other protein sources.
